To compute the expression 

\[
\frac{2^{10} - 2^8}{2^7 - 2^6},
\]

we will first calculate the values of \(2^{10}\), \(2^{8}\), \(2^{7}\), and \(2^{6}\) based on our previous calculations.

1. **Calculate \(2^{10}\)**:
   \[
   2^{10} = 1024
   \]

2. **Calculate \(2^{8}\)**:
   \[
   2^{8} = 256
   \]

3. **Calculate \(2^{7}\)**:
   \[
   2^{7} = 128
   \]

4. **Calculate \(2^{6}\)**:
   \[
   2^{6} = 64
   \]

Now, we can substitute these values into the expression:

### Step 1: Calculate the Numerator
\[
2^{10} - 2^{8} = 1024 - 256 = 768
\]

### Step 2: Calculate the Denominator
\[
2^{7} - 2^{6} = 128 - 64 = 64
\]

### Step 3: Substitute into the Expression
Now we substitute the results back into the expression:

\[
\frac{2^{10} - 2^{8}}{2^{7} - 2^{6}} = \frac{768}{64}
\]

### Step 4: Simplify the Fraction
Now we simplify \( \frac{768}{64} \):

\[
768 \div 64 = 12
\]

Thus, the final result of the computation is 

\[
\boxed{12}.
\] 

This is the simplest form of the expression.
