To solve the equation 

\[
\frac{6r^2 - 19r - 7}{2r - 7} = 4r - 3,
\]

we will follow these steps:

### Step 1: Eliminate the Fraction

Multiply both sides by \( (2r - 7) \):

\[
6r^2 - 19r - 7 = (4r - 3)(2r - 7).
\]

### Step 2: Expand the Right Side

Now, we expand the right-hand side:

\[
(4r - 3)(2r - 7) = 4r \cdot 2r - 4r \cdot 7 - 3 \cdot 2r + 3 \cdot 7.
\]

Calculating this gives:

\[
8r^2 - 28r - 6r + 21 = 8r^2 - 34r + 21.
\]

### Step 3: Set the Equation to Zero

Now, we set the equation to zero:

\[
6r^2 - 19r - 7 = 8r^2 - 34r + 21.
\]

Rearranging gives:

\[
6r^2 - 19r - 7 - 8r^2 + 34r - 21 = 0,
\]

which simplifies to:

\[
-2r^2 + 15r - 28 = 0.
\]

Multiplying through by -1 to make the leading coefficient positive:

\[
2r^2 - 15r + 28 = 0.
\]

### Step 4: Use the Quadratic Formula

Now, we can apply the quadratic formula:

\[
r = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a},
\]

where \( a = 2 \), \( b = -15 \), and \( c = 28 \).

Calculating the discriminant:

\[
b^2 - 4ac = (-15)^2 - 4 \cdot 2 \cdot 28 = 225 - 224 = 1.
\]

Now substituting into the quadratic formula:

\[
r = \frac{15 \pm \sqrt{1}}{2 \cdot 2} = \frac{15 \pm 1}{4}.
\]

This gives us two solutions:

1. \( r = \frac{16}{4} = 4 \)
2. \( r = \frac{14}{4} = \frac{7}{2} \)

### Step 5: Verify the Solutions

Now we will verify both solutions by substituting them back into the original equation.

**For \( r = 4 \)**:

Substituting \( r = 4 \):

\[
\frac{6(4)^2 - 19(4) - 7}{2(4) - 7} = \frac{96 - 76 - 7}{8 - 7} = \frac{13}{1} = 13,
\]

and 

\[
4(4) - 3 = 16 - 3 = 13.
\]

Both sides are equal, so \( r = 4 \) is a valid solution.

**For \( r = \frac{7}{2} \)**:

Substituting \( r = \frac{7}{2} \):

\[
\frac{6\left(\frac{7}{2}\right)^2 - 19\left(\frac{7}{2}\right) - 7}{2\left(\frac{7}{2}\right) - 7} = \frac{6 \cdot \frac{49}{4} - \frac{133}{2} - 7}{7 - 7} = \text{undefined}.
\]

The denominator becomes zero, so \( r = \frac{7}{2} \) is not a valid solution.

### Final Solution

The only valid solution to the equation 

\[
\frac{6r^2 - 19r - 7}{2r - 7} = 4r - 3
\]

is 

\[
\boxed{4}.
\]
